# CHAKRABOOST: Spatial Multipanner Phase Modulator

CHAKRABOOST is an 8-band spatial multipanner that combines three distinct DSP domains per band: phase modulation, dynamics processing, and spatial positioning. Each frequency band is aligned to a traditional chakra frequency range, from Root (20-63 Hz) through Transcendence (8000-20000 Hz).

Where most multiband processors treat bands as isolated gain stages, CHAKRABOOST operates as a **per-band spatial effects engine** — applying a vactrol-modeled phaser, adaptive parallel compression, and LFO-driven surround panning independently to each of the 8 chakra bands.

## The Processing Pipeline

Every audio band in CHAKRABOOST passes through three stages:

1. **Phase Modulation** — A 4-stage vactrol-modeled phaser per band, faithfully modeling the LED/LDR optocoupler behavior of the Ibanez FP-777 Flying Pan. Six standard LFO waveforms (sine, triangle, saw, reverse saw, square, random) plus a Neural LFO mode that uses a proprietary modulation system to produce organic, evolving modulation curves that escape the mechanical repetition of standard waveforms.

2. **Adaptive Compression** — Parallel compression per band with real-time crest-factor analysis. The compressor continuously adapts its behavior based on the dynamic profile of each band, blending compressed and dry signals for transparent density control without destroying transients.

3. **Spatial Panning** — Per-band stereo or surround positioning with LFO-driven orbital motion. Four speaker layouts are supported: Stereo, Quad, 5.1, and 7.1. Each band can follow its own spatial trajectory, creating immersive frequency-dependent movement across the soundfield.

CHAKRABOOST renders each band as a **Leadbeater-inspired chakra rosette** with the traditional petal counts from C. W. Leadbeater's descriptions of the energy centers. The rosettes are GPU-accelerated and respond in real-time to the audio level, phase modulation depth, and spatial position of each band.

## Key Capabilities

- **8 chakra-aligned frequency bands** from Root (20 Hz) through Transcendence (20 kHz)
- **4-stage vactrol-modeled phaser** per band with 6 LFO waveforms + Neural LFO mode (proprietary modulation)
- **Adaptive parallel compression** per band with crest-factor analysis
- **Spatial panning per band** across stereo, quad, 5.1, and 7.1 speaker layouts with LFO-driven orbits
- **True stereo FIR crossover** with linear-phase, 255-tap filter bank
- **GPU-accelerated Leadbeater rosette visualization** with real-time audio reactivity
- **Per-band solo/mute** and modulation lock for focused editing
- **~127 samples latency** (~2.9ms at 44.1kHz)
- **VST3, Audio Unit, and Standalone** formats on macOS
