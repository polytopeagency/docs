# Licensing

## Demo

PERF is available as a free demo with full functionality. The demo has no time limit and no feature restrictions.

## Purchase

Purchase a license at [nomn.ai](https://nomn.ai).

## Activation

After purchase, you'll receive a license key. Enter it in PERF's activation dialog (accessible from the plugin menu) to activate your copy.

## System Requirements

- **OS:** macOS (Apple Silicon + Intel)
- **Formats:** VST3, Audio Unit (AU), Standalone
- **DAW:** Any VST3 or AU-compatible host

## Support

For technical support, bug reports, or feature requests, contact us at [nomn.ai](https://nomn.ai).
