## Demo Mode

FOAM runs in full demo mode for 15 minutes per session. All features are available during the demo period.

## Purchasing

1. Visit [nomn.ai](https://nomn.ai)
2. Create an account
3. Navigate to FOAM and click **Buy Now**
4. Complete checkout via Stripe
5. Your license key appears immediately in your account dashboard

## Activation

1. In FOAM, click the **Activate** button
2. Enter your license key (format: `FOAM-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX`)
3. The plugin validates with nomn.ai and unlocks permanently

## Offline Use

After initial activation, FOAM works fully offline. Your license is stored locally and does not require an internet connection for subsequent launches.

## Multiple Machines

Each license supports up to 3 activations. If you need to move your license to a new machine, contact support.
